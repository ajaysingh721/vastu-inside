# Sample Videos for Vastu Website

To add videos to your website, you need to:

## Option 1: Use Your Own Videos
1. Record or obtain MP4 videos (15-30 seconds each)
2. Name them: vastu-1.mp4, vastu-2.mp4, vastu-3.mp4
3. Place them in this /public/videos/ folder
4. Update the page.tsx to use VideoCarousel component

## Option 2: Download Free Stock Videos
You can download free Vastu/architecture/home videos from:
- **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/videos/
- **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/videos/
- **Videvo**: https://www.videvo.net/

Search terms to use:
- "architecture"
- "house interior"
- "home design"
- "spiritual home"
- "meditation space"

## Option 3: Use YouTube Videos (Embedded)
Instead of video files, you can embed YouTube videos directly. Contact support if you need help implementing this.

## Video Specifications:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- File Size: Under 5MB per video
- Duration: 15-30 seconds recommended

## To Enable Video Carousel:
Once you have videos in this folder, uncomment the VideoCarousel component in app/page.tsx and replace the Image component.
